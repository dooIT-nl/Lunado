DeliveryMatch Shipping Integration

DeliveryMatch Odoo Integration automate and optimise your logistics process. From check-out to doorbell!

This integration will allow you to fully automate the selection and booking process to the carriers and external warehouses/fulfilment companies. DeliveryMatch is connected with 130 carriers worldwide.

DeliveryMatch can automatically select the best possible transport options based on costs, lead-time and CO2 output. During the operational process DeliveryMatch helps you to print all transport documents, like shipping labels, delivery-notes etc. and after the carrier booking, you can easily track and trace the shipments in Odoo or in the DeliveryMatch real-time data dashboard. By using the DeliveryMatch Odoo integration you can fully operate your logistics in your Odoo account.

All these features make your work efficient, ensures transport cost and CO2 reduction and uplift the customer experience. Interested in our software? please contact us.
